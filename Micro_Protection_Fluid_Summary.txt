THE PRODUCT IN ONE SENTENCE

Micro Protection Fluid is insurance that works like an ETF: you pay one fixed
premium (HKD 200/month), and AI automatically reallocates your coverage across
different risk categories based on what you're actually doing in real time.

============================================================================

THE KEY STRUCTURE

============================================================================

HKD 200 MONTHLY PREMIUM = TWO COMPONENTS:

┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  FIXED LIFE INSURANCE: HKD 90 (45%)                             │
│  - Death benefit: Multiplier 1:500 (max $45,000 at $90 alloc)   │
│  - Critical illness + disability                                │
│  - NEVER CHANGES (your family is always protected)              │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  DYNAMIC GENERAL INSURANCE: HKD 110 (55%)                       │
│  - Multipliers by category:                                     │
│    * Liability: 1:1,000 (max $90,000 at $90 alloc)              │
│    * Cyber: 1:200 (max $18,000 at $90 alloc)                    │
│    * Health: 1:200 (max $18,000 at $90 alloc)                   │
│    * Travel: 1:150 (max $13,500 at $90 alloc)                   │
│    * Property: 1:100 (max $9,000 at $90 alloc)                  │
│  - CONSTANTLY ADAPTS to your real-time life                     │
│  - AI reallocates this HKD 110 based on actual risk             │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

PAYOUT FORMULA: MIN(Actual Loss, Allocation × Multiplier)
Floors: $5 minimum per category | Ceilings: $90 maximum per category

RISK MITIGATION

============================================================================

CONCERN: "Won't there be a lot of payouts?"

RESPONSE: Yes, but our model ensures premiums exceed claims through:

1. Floor-and-ceiling: $5 minimum, $90 maximum per category
2. Payout formula: MIN(Actual Loss, Allocation × Multiplier)
3. Liquidity buffer: Tiered structure ($22 instant cash for parametric)
4. Real-time matching: Coverage aligned with actual risk
5. Reinsurance: 15% of premium ceded, covers catastrophic events
6. Scale: 500,000 customers = predictable claims (Law of Large Numbers)

LIQUIDITY TIERS FOR SUB-SECOND PAYOUTS:

Tier 1 (Instant):  $22 (20%) - Cash/overnight - Seconds - Parametric
Tier 2 (Same-day): $55 (50%) - T-bills/MMF - Hours - Travel, accident
Tier 3 (Reserve):  $33 (30%) - Ultra-short bonds - 1-2 days - Buffer
