Micro Protection Fluid
       ETF Style Adaptive Insurance for the Agentic Era
                — GBA Market Penetration —
1. Executive Summary
Our team proposes Micro Protection Fluid: an ETF-style adaptive
insurance solution that addresses the static coverage problem in the
agentic era. By combining agentic AI with real-time card authorization
data, merchant category codes, and supplementary signals like weather
and flight status, we aim to dynamically reallocate a fixed premium
across risk categories for HSBC Life customers while creating new
revenue opportunities in the GBA market.

2: Problem Statement & Opportunity
The problem: Insurance today is static. You pay a fixed premium, you
get fixed coverage, and it never adjusts whether you are sitting at home
or flying across Asia. You overpay for risks you do not face and
underpay for risks you do.
